About

My name is Tori Hall and I am currently a Senior Pricing Analyst for a major footwear retailer. I didn’t start in analytics but first found my home in politics. In my undergraduate career at The Ohio State University (Go Bucks!) I studied both qualitative and quantitative political science disciplines. I also tacked on Spanish and Creative Writing to satisfy an unending curiosity that has followed me my entire life. In these studies I pulled out two true big interests, which were storytelling and searching to understand the world around me in as many ways as possible. My quantitative political science studies first introduced me to a world of data science and analytics. To dig into this further I completed a master’s program in data science and I’m ready to hit the ground running. My focus on storytelling and my skills with cutting edge data science techniques give me a unique ability to made an impact on the world in a new way.

When I’m not at the computer I’m either boxing, weightlifting, reading or watching horror movies. Specifically the ones that are so bad they’re good. Feel free to reach out through any platforms listed on my contact page.
